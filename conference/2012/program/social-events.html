Hasso-Plattner-Institut Potsdam, Germany, March 25–30, 2012

Social Events

Workshop Reception (Monday 19:00)



View AOSD'12 Places in a larger map

The Workshop Reception takes place after the first day of workshops at the Hundertwasser Café next to the Dutch Quarter (Holländerviertel). There will be food and drinks for everyone, and the Reception is also the context in which the Student Research Competition's poster presentation takes place. Be sure to attend to see another part of Potsdam, and to meet up with other conference participants.

Getting there

Café Hundertwasser
Kurfürstenstraße 52
14467 Potsdam,
Germany

Bus shuttle

Public transportation

Public Transportation to Reception

Boat Trip (Wednesday 19:00)

On Wednesday evening, we will take you on a boat trip on the Havel lakes. You will see many beautiful and historical locations, including for example Glienicker Brücke (Glienicke Bridge), which is famous for the exchanges of captured spies having taken place in the Cold War period. Aboard the ship, a dinner buffet and drinks will be available.

Getting there

Schiffahrt in Potsdam

Use the bus shuttle directly from [Map] HPI to the point of depature and vice versa.

Banquet (Thursday 19:00)



View AOSD'12 Places in a larger map

The conference banquet takes place in close proximity to one of the most beautiful areas in Potsdam, Sanssouci Park. Take a walk around before the banquet and visit the famous terraces of Sanssouci Palace. The banquet speech is given by Crista Lopes. Also, the best paper will be honoured.

Getting there

Mövenpick Potsdam, Historische Mühle
Zur Historischen Mühle 2
14469 Potsdam,
Germany

Bus shuttle

Use the bus shuttle directly from [Map] HPI to the Mövenpick Restaurant and vice versa. On the way back, the bus will additionally stop at the Mercure Hotel [Map].

Public transportation

Public Transportation to Banquet

Ice Cream Event (Friday 17:30)

Right after the main conference, there will be ice cream in the foyer of the lecture building.

Additional information

Below, select the conference fee and/or the tutorials you'd like to attend.

An Introductory Tutorial Package consisting of four tutorials (T1, T2, T3 and T6), covering the major AOSD approaches, is available for the price of three).

The conference fee includes the proceedings, lunches on April 24-26, refreshments during the breaks and the banquet dinner on April 25. The tutorial fee includes the tutorial notes, lunch on the day of the tutorial, and refreshments during the breaks.

Workshop attendance is by invitation only. Workshop attendance is included in the conference fee; workshop participants must register for the conference. Lunch tickets (€ 14 per person per day) for April 22 and 23 can be purchased.

The student conference fee does not include the banquet. Extra banquet tickets (€ 75 per ticket) can be purchased.

An extremely limited number of discounts are available for persons for whom the conference fee represents a significant burden. Please contact the organization and provide a brief description of your circumstances, and we will reply to you by email.

To pay by credit card, print the feedback message you get after registering, complete the credit card information, and return it to us by fax (preferably) or mail immediately.

Registration is subject to receipt of payment.
